THE BEST QUESADILLA

DESCRIPTION

The perfect chicken quesdailla to make at home.
Roast up your own chicken breast or make it even easier on yourself
by using a prepared rotisserie chicken.

INGREDIENTS(8)

1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese (about 2 ounces)
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese (about 2 ounces)
4 (10-inch) flour tortillas
1 1/2 cups shredded, cooked chicken (about 8 ounces)
2 tablespoons coarsely chopped fresh cilantro
Guacamole (optional, see above)
Salsa, for serving (optional, see above)
Sour cream, for serving (optional)

DESCRIPTION

1. - Combine the cheeses in a medium bowl.

2. - Heat a large frying pan over medium heat until hot,
about 3 minutes. Place a tortilla in the pan and sprinkle
with half of the chicken, half of the cilantro, and and half
of the cheese mixture. Top with a second tortilla and cook
until the underside of the bottom tortilla is golden brown
in several spots and half of the cheese is melted, about 3
minutes. Using a spatula, carefully flip the quesadilla over
and cook until the underside of the second tortilla is crisp
and golden brown in several spots and all of the cheese is
melted, about 2 to 3 minutes more.

3.- Slide the quesadilla from the pan onto a cutting board
and cut into wedges. Repeat with the remaining ingredients
to make a second quesadilla. Serve topped with guacamole,
salsa, and sour cream, if desired.
